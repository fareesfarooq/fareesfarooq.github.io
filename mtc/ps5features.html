Specifications

Performance and Graphics

The PlayStation 5 is powered by a custom AMD Zen 2 processor with 8 cores running at 3.5 GHz, delivering exceptional performance for next-generation gaming. It features a custom RDNA 2 GPU from AMD, providing 10.28 teraflops of power to ensure smooth and visually stunning gameplay at 4K resolution.

Memory and Storage

The PS5 comes with 16 GB of GDDR6 memory, offering increased bandwidth and faster load times for a seamless gaming experience. Its ultra-fast 825 GB SSD significantly reduces load times compared to traditional hard drives and supports faster game installations and performance.

Audio and Compatibility

The PS5 introduces 3D audio technology for a more immersive sound experience, enhancing spatial awareness in supported games. It is also backward compatible with the majority of PS4 games, allowing users to enjoy their existing library with improved performance and faster load times.

Controller

The new DualSense wireless controller offers advanced haptic feedback, adaptive triggers, and a built-in microphone, providing a more immersive and responsive gaming experience.
